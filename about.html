ABOUT TOURISTICA

Welcome To TOURISTICA

Introducing Touristicia, your premier travel agency for unforgettable journeys across Canada, the USA, and South America. Founded with a passion for exploration and a commitment to excellence, Touristicia invites you to embark on fully-escorted bus tours that combine luxury, convenience, and cultural immersion. Building on years of experience and expertise, Touristicia has curated an array of meticulously planned itineraries, ensuring every traveler experiences the beauty and excitement of each destination with ease.

Since our establishment, Touristicia has been dedicated to providing exceptional travel experiences. Our journey began in 2004 with a focus on showcasing Canada's most iconic cities like Montreal, Quebec City, and Ottawa. Over the years, we expanded our horizons to include the vibrant landscapes of the United States, offering luxury bus tours to cities such as New York City, Boston, and Washington D.C. Departing from convenient locations in the Greater Toronto Area, our tours promise unparalleled comfort and exploration.

At Touristicia, we understand that every traveler seeks a unique experience. That's why we offer a diverse range of tour packages, including immersive visits to Niagara Falls and Toronto. Whether you're seeking a day trip or an overnight adventure, our packages cater to all preferences, providing access to Ontario's must-see attractions in the company of knowledgeable guides and aboard our luxurious coaches. With convenient pick-up locations from Downtown Toronto to Airport hotels, your journey with Touristicia begins with ease and comfort.

Touristicia invites you to explore the enchanting beauty of South America, from the historic allure of Buenos Aires to the breathtaking landscapes of Machu Picchu. Embark on meticulously planned tours that promise unforgettable experiences in destinations like Rio de Janeiro, Lima, and Santiago. With first-class accommodations, expert guidance, and immersive itineraries, Touristicia ensures every moment of your journey is filled with wonder and delight.

Join us on an adventure through South America's diverse landscapes with our fully-escorted 7-Day South American Tour. Experience the vibrant energy of Rio de Janeiro, the ancient mysteries of Machu Picchu, and the cultural richness of Buenos Aires. With guided city tours, deluxe accommodations, and seamless transportation, our South American tours embody the excellence and dedication that define Touristicia's standards.

At Touristicia, our commitment to excellence extends beyond destinations; it encompasses every aspect of your journey. With professional tour directors leading the way, we promise to create a travel experience that is both enriching and enjoyable for everyone on board. Join us and embark on a journey of discovery with Touristicia, where every moment is crafted to exceed your expectations. Welcome aboard!
